# Code of Conduct

## The Short Version

Be kind. Be inclusive. Don't be an asshole.

## The Slightly Longer Version

Everyone interacting with this project — whether filing issues, submitting PRs, commenting, or just hanging out — deserves to feel welcome regardless of who they are, where they come from, or what they know.

- **Be chill.** People make mistakes, ask basic questions, and have bad days. This project is supposed to be helpful, so be helpful.
- **Be gay, do crimes.** Inclusivity means everyone: all backgrounds, identities, experience levels.
- **Be constructive.** Disagree with ideas, not people. "That approach has a problem because X" beats "that's stupid" every time.
- **Don't be a dick.** Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and general toxicity have no place here. Cussing in general frustration isn't harassment, cussing _at someone_ might be. You know what this means. Don't make us spell it out.

## Enforcement

If someone is being an asshole, let the maintainer know if they don't already by emailing jlop8ib0x@mozmail.com. We'll do our best to handle it. Repeated or egregious behavior gets you shown the door.
